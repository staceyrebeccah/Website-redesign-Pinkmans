Order


You can pre-order your food to collect or be delivered to your door. We require orders by 12.00 for next day (Monday - Saturday). To order food for collection or delivery, please use our online shop. Alternatively, you can email us at specialorder@pinkmans.co.uk along with details of your order. Delivery charges apply - please see here for more info.

You can also order your Pinkmans favourites individually on Deliveroo and have them delivered directly to you at short notice.
